    Sponsors

    The Minister has been working hard to expand the programme to reach more girls in addition to the funds provided by the government.

    She has set out to raise funds from benevolent individuals and organisations to enable more girls to be trained under the programme. Since its inception, the programme has been funded entirely by the Ministry of Communications and Digitalisation (MoCD), National Communications Authority (NCA) and Ghana Investment Fund for Electronic Communications (GIFEC) with some support from network operators and the American Tower Corporation (ATC).
    However, the funds have not been enough to scale up the programme.
